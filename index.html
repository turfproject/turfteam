I started Vitrified Studio in the spring of 2011 in my backyard in Portland, Oregan. I love simple handmade ceramics, and I want to share what I make with other people so they can enjoy it too. All of my work is simple, utilitarian wheel thrown ceramics, made of five types of stoneware or porcelain fired to cone 4-5 in an electric kiln.
